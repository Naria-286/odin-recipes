Meatballs

Description

The easiest homemade meatball recipe ever. Super easy with only 3 ingredients. It can even be made in under 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (453g) 70% Lean Ground Beef
  • 2 tablespoons (15g) dry mushroom soup mix
  • 1 slice stale bread, chopped

Steps

    Prep

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).

    2. Meatballs

  2. In a large bowl mix together the ground beef, soup mix, stale bread, and a splash of water until fully combined.
  3. On to a parchment paper-lined sheet tray, divide the mixture into 8 portions and roll into balls.
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the meatballs are cooked through and nicely browned on the outside

    5. Serve

  5. Enjoy your super quick 3-ingredient meatballs!
